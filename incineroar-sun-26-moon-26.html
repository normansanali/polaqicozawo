Incineroar (Japanese: ガオガエン Gaogaen) is a Fire-type Stage 2 Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Sun & Moon expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Sun & Moon expansion, released in Japan as a Water Web Holofoil SM-P Promo. The Japanese print was made available in the Incineroar Special Set, released January 27, 2017. An exclusive Cracked Ice Holofoil print was included in the English Roaring Heat Theme Deck. It was reprinted in Japan as another SM-P Promotional card, available as part of the Midsummer's Pika Pika Alola Festival, which ran from July 22 to September 3, 2017. Customers at participating Pokémon Centers (including the Pokémon Center Online) and Pokémon Stores who purchased five booster packs in one transaction were given 1 of 6 possible promotional cards. This print features the Pokémon Center logo in the bottom right corner of the artwork.

A Water Web Holofoil variant of the English print was made available in February 2020 in the Incineroar Sun & Moon GX Challenge Box.

Trivia

Origin

Fire Fang and Darkest Lariat are both moves in the Pokémon games that Incineroar can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sun.
