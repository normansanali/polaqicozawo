Escape From Tarkov &quot;The Punisher

July 2024 · 3 minute read

The Punisher – Part 4 is a quest in Escape From Tarkov given by Prapor. It unlocks after completing The Punisher – Part 3. The quest leads to The Punisher – Part 5.

Escape From Tarkov The Punisher – Part 4 Quest Guide

The Punisher – Part 4 Quest Dialogue

Hey, your last Scav cutting was off the chain! People keep coming and asking what the heck is going on. Turns out that my friends somehow negotiated with the local authorities – what’s left of them anyway – to set up some kind of mercenary fights, like gladiator battles. Can you imagine that? We just keep falling back to the Middle Ages with all this shit! In short, for them you are, what’s it called… a game tester, sort of. Whatever they are coming up with, they reevaluate and adjust it according to your actions. Long story short, I don’t really understand shit, but they suggest us to continue. This time you will have a way more serious enemy, they want you to decimate PMCs, but you should be kinda disguised as a Scav, so cover your face with a balaclava and put on their jacket, at least. And also, you are supposed to scare off the real Scavs – using a 12 gauge shotgun. It’s almost as if they want to start WW3. Stirring shit up and making others turn on each other… Are you in?

Objectives

  • Eliminate 10 Scavs while using a 12ga shotgun on Woods
  • Eliminate PMCs while wearing a balaclava (any type) and Scav vest on Shoreline
  • Find 5 Bars A-2607 knives in raid (those with brown handle)
  • Hand over the knives

Rewards

  • 18000XP
  • +0.03 reputation with Prapor
  • Unknown Reward (Colt M4A1 5.56×45 assault rifle (Variant SOPMOD II)
  • ~115000 Roubles
  • Unlocks the purchase of 5.45x39mm BT gs

Reward Dialogue

Mighty wicked, warrior. Grab your reward. Come back later, my friends have got some more work for you.

The Punisher – Part 4 Quest Guide

This quest requires you to do multiple things and will definitely take you multiple runs on some of the maps listed above. First, you need to head to Woods and eliminate 10 Scavs while using a 12ga shotgun. Second, you need to Eliminate 10 PMCs while wearing a balaclava (any type) and Scav Vest on Shoreline. Last, you need to find 5 A-2607 knives (the ones with brown handles) and return them to Prapor.

Which weapons can you use to eliminate scavs on Woods?

  • Saiga 12ga (semi-auto)
  • MP-153 (semi-auto)
  • MP-155 (semi-auto)
  • M870 (pump)
  • MP-133 (pump)
  • 590A1 12ga (pump)

Where to find Bars A-2607 knives in raid?

  • The easiest way to gather A-2607 knives in raid is by looting Scavs. Therefore, make sure you loot your AI kills.

Scav Vest and Balaclava?

  • You can buy Scav Vest from Jaeger for one Slickers bar (Barter) instead of buying it from the FLEA Market. It should be a lot cheaper.
  • Balaclava can be bought from Ragman, whichever suits you the best.
