Who Has Kristen Stewart Dated? A History of Her Loves

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Ah, celebrity love. Every little change in a relationship is big news in Hollywood, and when it comes to someone’s favorite actor or actress, fans tend to like being in the know about their love life. Kristen Stewart has had her share of high-profile relationships her fans have been rabid about, as well as more low-key couplings that she tried to keep private.

The Spencer actress seems to have put her dating days behind her, as she revealed on November 2, 2021, that she’s engaged to her girlfriend of two years, screenwriter Dylan Meyer.

“We’re marrying, we’re totally gonna do it,” Kristen shared during an appearance on The Howard Stern Show. She added, “I wanted to be proposed to, so, I think I very distinctly carved out what I wanted, and she nailed it. We’re marrying, it’s happening.”

Two years ago on the same show, the Personal Shopper star revealed she was ready to marry Dylan after only three months of dating. “I want to be, like, sort of somewhat reasonable about it, but I think good things happen fast,” Kristen said the time of how she “couldn’t wait” to propose to her new love. She added, “I have a couple plans that are just the coolest things to do. I’m really impulsive. I don’t know when it’s going to be.”

Kristen and Dylan were first spotted as a couple when they were photographed in August 2019, making out on a New York City stoop only a month after the actress and model Stella Maxwell broke up for the final time. It followed a romantic vacation to Italy for K-Stew and the Victoria’s Secret Angel, who had been on and off for several years.

Of course, Kristen’s most famous romance to date was her five-year union with her fellow Twilight star Robert Pattinson. During her 2019 Howard Stern appearance, he asked Kristen if she ever thought the two would end up married.

“I don’t know. I wanted to … yeah, no, I’ve never been in…” Kristen began but seemed to grasp for words. Howard then nudged her by saying, “If he proposed, you would’ve gotten married.”

“I don’t know,” Stewart explained. “I’m not a super duper traditionalist, but at the same time … every relationship I’ve ever been in, I thought that was it. I’ve never really been the most casual person.” Now, she’s found her relationship that really is “it.”
