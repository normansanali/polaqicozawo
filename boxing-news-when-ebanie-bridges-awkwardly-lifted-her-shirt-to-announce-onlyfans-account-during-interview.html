Ebanie Bridges has a disappointing opinion about fighting in the UFC

The women’s bantamweight champion under the IBF might be criticized for her tendencies to appeal to her audience through her looks, but that’s not all the Australian has. No one can deny the respect that Ebanie has earned through her performances in the ring against formidable opponents. Naturally, this compels fans to wonder if she’ll pursue other combat sports formats like MMA.

Ebanie Bridges states that she fancies“getting hit in the face with an elbow or a knee.” She explains how she is content, boxing with her gloves on rather than endangering herself by increasing the chances of her ankle or hand getting snapped. Ebanie also clarifies that MMA is a much more brutal sport and gives “hats off” to people who participate in it.

Having an elite boxer like Bridges in the UFC is a fascinating idea. Her presence in the bantamweight division would intrigue many fighting enthusiasts. But hearing about her disinterest in getting involved with the Octagon can only disappoint many fight fans worldwide.
