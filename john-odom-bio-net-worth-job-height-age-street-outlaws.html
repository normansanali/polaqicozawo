How Did John Odom Make His Money?

John Odom founded HMH Construction, Odom Racing, and Skyline Homes Development. Since then, Odom has led multiple residential and commercial projects such as Crescent Lakes, Thunder Ridge Lakes of Telaga II, and Scarlett Ridge.

The HMH Construction website says, “John Odom has worked in the Excavation & Construction industry for 20 + years, gaining experience in all things concrete, construction, excavation, utilities, and development. Being a Native of Idaho, born in Twin Falls, he is extremely passionate about growth in the Treasure Valley and being a part of the huge boom in Housing Developments.”

Along with Dale Newberry, Odom owns and operates Skyline Homes & Developments, a business that specializes in creating residential communities. The company has purchased land to create divisions and has projects in at least seven regions. The Crescent Lakes subdivision in the Middleton area of Idaho is now under development for 103 single-family building lots.

The subdivision is based in Treasure Valley, which according to its website is one of the top 100 finest places to live in the world.

John and his wife are also “relative newcomers to the Republican donor scene” and gave $100,000 to the pro-Trump super PAC that Lewandowski was running, according to Politico. Federal Election Commission records show the Odoms’ construction company, HMH Construction LLC, contributed $60,000 to the PAC in June 2021. The FEC records show contributions made to the MAGA Action PAC up until August 2021.

John Odom Parents

John Odom was born to his late father Robert Odom and Marchella J Odom. His parents stayed happily married till his mother’s death. Then his father married his second wife Marilyn Rowell Odom for 13 years until Robert’s death. Robert was a native of Arizona but spent the majority of his life in Twin Falls, Idaho before moving to Washington Parish over a decade ago.

Robert was a United States Army veteran of the Vietnam War Era. He enjoyed reading Western novels and playing card games. He also had a large collection of antique model cars. Moreover, he was a member of the Faith Tabernacle Apostolic Church.

John’s siblings are Trampas Cooksey (wife Sharon), Josh Cooksey (Destiny), and Brian Cooksey of Tylertown, MS, Daughter and Son in law: Amy and Michael Thomas of Tylertown.

Is John Odom Married?

Yes, John Odom is a married man. His wife is Trashelle Odom. They exchanged their wedding vows on 7 July 2017. She and her husband are residing in Meridian, Idaho.

On 2 August 2021, John wished his wife her birthday writing, “Happy Birthday to my best friend and companion on this crazy journey we call our life. Thank you for everything you do for me and our incredible family. I definitely couldn’t do this all without you.”

The couple is now parents to five children; three sons Holland, Hudson, Mason, and two daughters Makaylan and Scarlett Odom. Hudson recently celebrated his birthday on 21 April 2022. Holland celebrated him on 12 June.

They read: “Outside of the office, John enjoys raising his family which includes 5 kids! Ranging from 1 to 19 years old. He is non-stop, and he loves to work being on site some days moving equipment or staking areas by 5 am before his crews have even arrived. He visits all projects throughout the day, ensuring things are running smoothly, He is not afraid to get dirty to get things done, and he is the farthest thing from a procrastinator.”

Before John, Trashelle was married to her ex-husband Chase McGregor. In December 2011, they welcomed a son named Mason George Alan on 21 December 2011.

John Odom Height
