Véronique Zidane is the spouse of Zinedine Zidane, a popular administrator of La Liga club, Real Madrid. She was born on November 10, 1972 in Rodez, Aveyron. She is the daughter Anthonio Fernandez Lentisco and Ana Ramirez Martinez.

Veronique wanted to teach dance when she was young and to achieve this, she dropped out of school to achieve the goal. However, she met her future husband, Zidane while she was learning dance at the Roselle Hightower Dance School in Cannes.

Veronique realised how important it was for her to support Zidane’s fame so she ditched her dancing and modelling aspirations and accompanied him to Cannes until they finally settled in Madrid.
