Biz Markie’s Early Life

Biz Markie was born in Harlem, New York, and raised in Long Island. He began his music career in Manhattan nightclubs and later performed at colleges. His unique style and charisma quickly caught the attention of the music industry.

Markie’s debut album, “Goin’ Off,” was released in 1988, featuring the hit singles “Make the Music with Your Mouth, Biz” and “Nobody Beats the Biz.” His second album, “The Biz Never Sleeps,” was released in 1989 and included his most successful single, “Just a Friend.”

Continued Success in Music

Despite legal issues that affected his career in the 1990s, Markie continued to release albums and singles. His unique style and humorous lyrics have kept him relevant in the music industry for over three decades.

Acting and Television Career
