Khloe Posts Pic of Diamond Rings After Kims Robbery

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Khloé Kardashian shared a photo of her and new beau Tristan Thompson’s matching diamond rings, but some fans thought it was insensitive and possibly dangerous for the reality star to post pics of her bling after her sister Kim Kardashian’s robbery. 

The Strong Looks Better Naked author, 32, cheered on the basketball player, 25, as he received his NBA championship ring and played in the Cleveland Cavaliers’ season opener on Tuesday, October 25.

Afterward, the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star snapped an Instagram photo of her hand on top of the athlete's, showing off their jewelry. Thompson wore his massive new ring, which features a Cavaliers logo surrounded by diamonds, while Kardashian stacked three diamond bands along with a fun red and gold ring in the team’s colors.

Fans on social media worried that Khloé or Thompson could be targeted next since armed robbers stole an estimated $10 million worth of Kim’s jewelry after several posts about her new engagement ring on social media. “Didn’t Kim just get robbed for doing this,” one commenter wrote, while another added, “Apparently you didn’t learn anything from what happened to your sister. You should be careful what you post.”

Despite the backlash, Kardashian seemed to enjoy her night out in Ohio. The Good American Denim designer, who reached a divorce settlement with ex Lamar Odom earlier this week, and the NBA center have been going strong since mid-September. Their relationship has already overcome a few bumps too. In early October, news broke that Thompson is expecting a baby with his ex-girlfriend, Jordy C, but that didn’t deter the reality star from pursuing the romance. A source previously told Us, “She’s trying to let go and have fun."
