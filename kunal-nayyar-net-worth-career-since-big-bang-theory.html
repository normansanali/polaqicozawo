Kunal Nayyar, the talented British-Indian actor, has experienced an extraordinary surge in his net worth following the conclusion of the immensely popular TV series, The Big Bang Theory. Known for his portrayal of Rajesh Koothrappali, Nayyar’s journey through the show was marked by unique aspects that contributed to his financial success.

When The Big Bang Theory concluded, some actors expressed mixed feelings about the show’s ending. However, Nayyar wasn’t one of them. He embraced the conclusion wholeheartedly, stating, “I think it’s a beautiful way to end.” The actor’s positivity and gratitude for the show’s impact on his career and life reflected his professionalism and appreciation for the opportunity he had been given.

7 How Much Did Kunal Nayyar Earn From The Big Bang Theory?

Estimated Net Worth Boost: At least $20 million

  • Starting salary: $45,000
  • Final salary: $1 million

When Kunal Nayyar first joined The Big Bang Theory, he started with a salary of $45,000 per episode. However, as the show’s popularity soared, so did his earnings. By the time the series reached its conclusion, he was earning a remarkable $1 million for each episode, on a par with the best-paid cast members on the sitcom.

This substantial increase in his per-episode salary showcased not only the show’s massive success, but also the significant financial gains that came with it. Over the course of the series, which spanned 12 seasons and featured over 280 episodes, Kunal Nayyar’s income from The Big Bang Theory served as a substantial pillar of his continued financial success.

6 Kunal Nayyar Earned A Fortune From The Trolls Franchise

Estimated Net Worth Boost: At least $5 million

  • Trolls at the box office: $346 million
  • Trolls World Tour digital release earnings: $288 million
  • Trolls Band Together digital release earnings: unknown

Other than The Big Bang Theory, another one of Kunal Nayyar’s most prominent acting credits is in the 2016 animated comedy-musical Trolls, as well as its two sequels, Trolls World Tour (2020) and Trolls Band Together (2023). In the first installment, Nayyar lent his voice to the character of Guy Diamond, “a glittery Troll with a highly auto-tuned voice.” The movie, featuring an ensemble cast, grossed an impressive $346 million worldwide.

The second movie, Trolls World Tour was notable for its innovative digital release due to the COVID-19 pandemic, making it accessible to a wide audience. The film's unique distribution strategy paid off, earning approximately $193 million in the United States, and over $288 million globally.
