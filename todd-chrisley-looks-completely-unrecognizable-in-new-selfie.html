Todd Chrisley Looks Completely Unrecognizable In New Selfie

August 2024 Â· 2 minute read

In the hours following the selfie post (pictured left), Todd Chrisley's Instagram got flooded with comments from fans and stars alike. "You look 12," Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star Lisa Rinna commented. Real Housewives of Orange County alum, Tamra Judge, said he looked slightly older, quipping, "You look 18."

Many fans also chimed in, wanting to know the secret to his youthful appearance. Page Six reported that one fan went so far as to ask if the new face was from a filter or a doctor. "Did you use filters on this photo or have you had a facelift?" they said. Someone else penned, "That isn't your face love the hell out of you Todd but we know what you look like and this anit [sic] it please don't be another Khloe Kardashian." Other fans were more complimentary, with one commenter writing, "Idk what you're doing, but keep it up."

Chrisley denied any enhancements, either digital or medical. He did respond to a few comments by clarifying what anti-aging procedures he uses to keep his face so fresh and glowy, though. Chrisley owned up to getting botox "every six months," and to regularly scheduling hydro facials and halo laser treatments. Whether that's all there is, or there are some other invasive procedures he's not sharing, remains to be seen. But when it comes to skincare (or maybe filters) it looks like Todd Chrisley really does know best.
