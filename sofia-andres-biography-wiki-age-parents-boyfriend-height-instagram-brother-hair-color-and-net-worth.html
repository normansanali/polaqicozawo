Sofia Andres Biography – Sofia Andres Wiki

Sofia Andres full name Sofia Louise Alejandre Andres, is a Filipina actress and model. She was born on 24 August 1998 in Antipolo, Philippines.

Andres completed her high school education at ADT Montessori School located in Pasig on 26 March 2015.

She begun her career as a commercial model and modelled for brands such as Angel Evaporada and KFC.

Sofia Andres Awards

She has won various awards such as New Movie Actress of the Year for her role in Relaks, It’s Just Pag-Ibig, and Female Star of the Night in PMPC Star Awards for Movies in 2015. Also in 2015 in GMMSF Box-Office Entertainment Awards, she won the Most Promising Loveteam with Iñigo Pascual for their role in Relaks, It’s Just Pag-Ibig, during the 63rd FAMAS Awards in 2015 she won the German Moreno Youth Achievement Award and that same year in 29th PMPC Star Awards for Television, she won the Best Supporting Actress for her role in Forevermore. In 2016, she was in Yes! Magazine’s 100 Most Beautiful Stars and Couple of the Night with Diego Loyzaga in Star Magic Ball Awards.
