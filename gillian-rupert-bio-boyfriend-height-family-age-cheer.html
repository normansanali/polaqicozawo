Who Is Cheer's Gillian Rupert? Her Bio, Boyfriend, Height, Family, Age

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Meet Gillian Rupert, best friend, and teammate of Cassadee Dunlap. She has been cheering with Navarro since 2019 and is now appearing on Netflix’s show ‘Cheer’ in season 2. Here, you’ll learn about her age, boyfriend, height, and family.

Tag along with this Gillian Rupert Bio that unfolds information as it proceeds.

Gillian Rupert On Netlix’s Cheer Season 2

Gillian Rupert has been with Navarro college for the year ’19-20′ and ’20-21′ teams. On the show, she talks about her childhood and relationship with her parents. She is one of the new faces that we’re going to show in season 2 of the show.

With the Navarro College cheerleaders, the Emmy-winning docuseries return. The show’s first season premiered in January 2020. As a result of the show, Navarro’s coach, Monica Aldama, and the rest of her team became celebrities.

Jerry Harris, his arrest, and the aftermath of his arrest will also be the center of the 2022 season, according to the show’s second season trailer. After the first season’s success, the second season of ‘Cheer’ began filming in secret in January 2020. The coach and cheerleading squad from Navarro’s arch-rival Trinity Valley Community College are also featured in the new season.

Because of the COVID-19 outbreak, production was halted in March. Filming, however, resumed in September 2020. Viewers will remember Monica Aldama, Lexi Brumback, Gabi Butler, La’Darius Marshall, and Morgan Simianer from past seasons in addition to the new castings. In its second season, the show contains nine episodes. This time, however, things are different. Navarro must defend their championship against Trinity Valley Community College this time.

From early 2020 until April 2021, the events surrounding Jerry Harris are recorded in the series, with the charges against him being covered in episode five. On the show, the brothers who accused Jerry of sexual communication will also tell their stories.

Other newbies besides Cassadee are Brooke Morosca, Gillian Rupert, Maddy Brum, and Payton Sykes.

Fun Fact: Gillian Rupert started GoFundMe to help pay for UCA National Collegiate Championship for which she and her cheer partner Ty Johnson was selected. The Show is going to be held on 14-16 of January 2022 in Orlando, Florida.

Talking about her boyfriend, David is an up-and-coming baseball player at National Park College. While searching for a college, he knew he wanted to continue playing baseball but had not yet received an offer.

David met NPC Baseball head coach Richard Thompson in the summer of 2020, who offered Newsom a scholarship and NPC became his college home.

Gillian Rupert Family

Gillian Rupert was born to her mother Angela K Rupert. Angela and her father either separated or already divorced. While Angela actively involves herself in Gillian’s life, there are no traces of her father. Angela appears to be in a relationship with a man named Ron Gallegos.

Angela attended North Mesquite High School and Poteet High School. She worked as Head Assistant at Dr. James Archambeau. She also worked as warehouse manager at Cheer Athletics.

Gillian is also close to her maternal grandparents Robert Keesecker and Lilly Teresa Holloway Keesecker. Her grandparents married on 24 April 1971 and they have been together ever since. Her grandfather attended Brownsville High School, Brownsville, Texas, and graduated in 1964.

He then studied accounting and pre-law at Texas A&M University.
