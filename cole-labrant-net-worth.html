Cole LaBrant Wiki Biography

Cole LaBrant was born on 21 August 1996, in Troy, Alabama USA, and is a social media personality, YouTuber, and actor, best known for his Vine channel which has grown to popularity, amassing more than six million followers. He’s been active online since 2012, and all of his efforts have helped put his net worth to where it is today.

How rich is Cole LaBrant? As of late-2017, sources estimate a net worth that is at $2.5 million, mostly earned through success in his various online endeavors. He earns large amounts of money through his content on Vine, and is also a part of the collaboration group called “Dem White Boyz”. As he continues his efforts, it is expected that his wealth will also continue to increase.
