JJ Wolf Parents & Family Life Details

Born on 21 December 1998, Wolf is 23 years old now. His birth name is Jeffrey John Wolf.

JJ is the youngest to two children of parents Jeff Wolf and Brooke Wolf. He has an elder sister named Danielle Wolf.


Well, sports run in the blood of the Wolf family. His father Jeff played professional tennis in the early 90s and his mother Brooke played tennis at Miami University.


Moreover, his sister Danielle played collegiate tennis at The Ohio State University. She now serves as a High Performance Supervisor of Junior Tennis at Midtown Athletic Clubs.

JJ’s grandfather Charley Wolf played professional baseball for the Cincinnati Reds and coached in the National Basketball Association for the Detroit Pistons and Cincinnati Royals.

His Career Journey

Wolf started playing semi-pro tennis in 2016. In the same year, he committed to playing collegiate tennis at The Ohio State University.

In 2019, the six-foot turned professional and signed with Topnotch Management. Moreover, he won the 2019 Champaign Challenger, defeating Sebastian Korda. He began the year 2020 by winning the ATP Challenger Tour.


JJ made his Grand Slam singles debut at the 2020 U.S. Open. However, he lost in the third round to Russian Daniil Medvedev 6–3, 6–3, 6–2.
