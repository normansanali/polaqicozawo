Who is Lefty SM's wife Maria Isabel?

September 2024 · 3 minute read

MEXICAN rapper Lefty SM has tragically died with reports claiming he was shot.

The 31-year-old artist has left a loving family behind, including wife Maria Isabel, here's everything you need to know about her.

Who is Lefty SM's wife?

Rapper Lefty SM was married to Maria Isabel, also known as Eza Mary.

The couple has two daughters together, Keysie and Ciara.

According to her Instagram, ezamaria_, which has over 200,000 followers as of September 2023, Eza is a fitness model and fashion enthusiast.

The widow updated her profile picture with a black bow, reportedly in solidarity of her husband's death.

The influencer regularly posts snaps of happier times celebrating their daughters' birthdays.

The celebrity duo also owns a clothing brand, 50 grados, featuring exclusive Lefty SM merchandise.

According to La Razon, Eza Mary took her husband to hospital after the tragic shooting.

The widow asked her fans to respect the family's private mourning period on heartbreaking Facebook post, according to La Razon Mexico.

On the English translated statement Eza said: "Don't send messages. I don't want to hear from anyone. I'm just having a bad dream. Pinch me my love, I want to get out of this nightmare, please."

How did Lefty SM die?

Lefty SM was shot to death in Zapopan, Jalisco, in Mexico at the age of 31 on Sunday, September 3, 2023.

According to local reports, he was attacked near his home in the neighborhood of La Cima in Zapopan.

Alzada Records confirmed the artist's tragic death on Instagram.

The English translation of the post reads: "Dear Alzada friends and family, with profound sadness we’re informing you about the death of our brother, Lefty SM, Juan Carlos Sauceda.

"Our love and prayers are with his wife María Isabel and their two daughters."

Carlos Félix, a PR rep for Alzada Records, told Billboard Español that the rapper was shot twice and declared dead at a local hospital.

Several fellow artists sent tributes to the late rapper sharing their condolences.

Santa Fe Klan paid tribute to his former collaborator on Instagram by writing in Spanish with a picture of the pair, "It's not possible...Tell me it's a [lie]."

Jalisco authorities are yet to comment on Lefty SM's death.

What was Lefty SM's real name?

Lefty SM was born Juan Carlos Sauceda in San Luis Río Colorado, Mexico, on April 22, 1992.

He was a rapper signed by the label Alzada Records.
