FIFA 23: How to complete the Marcel Sabitzer Fantasy FUT SBC

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Players may get the Team 2 cards in FIFA 23 Ultimate Team via the ongoing promotion, which coincides with the release of the Marcel Sabitzer Fantasy FUT SBC. The mentioned item won’t involve opening any packs, unlike the cards that were made available for the main team.

The only requirements for players are the specified SBC’s tasks. If they finish all the tasks in the specified time, players will get a guaranteed promo card. For those that don’t have to rely on their luck in the first place, this makes it rather exciting. Also, there is the opportunity for players to buy it for a lower cost.

Related: FIFA 23: How to complete the Mario Gomez Fantasy FUT Player Pick SBC

The Marcel Sabitzer Fantasy FUT SBC is live in FIFA 23 Ultimate Team

The Marcel Sabitzer Fantasy FUT SBC is a bit pricey because EA Sports includes four tasks with it. The given conditions for each task define how much it will cost to complete. To unlock the card, FIFA 23 players must finish all the tasks within the allocated period.

Requirements to complete the Marcel Sabitzer Fantasy FUT SBC:

Manchester Utd

  • Min. 1 Player from Manchester Utd
  • Min. Team Rating: 82

Premier League

  • Min. 1 Player from Premier League
  • Min. Team Rating: 84

Top form

  • Min. 1 Players: Team of the Week
  • Min. Team Rating: 85

86 rated squad

  • Min. Team Rating: 86

If players purchase every piece of fodder from the market, the SBC will cost roughly 285,000 FUT coins to complete. Players can grind game modes like Squad Battles and Division Rivals to earn extra cards because it’s only accessible for a week. The final cost of completion can be decreased with these resources.

Up until March 17, the Marcel Sabitzer Fantasy FUT SBC will be accessible, allowing FIFA 23 gamers to benefit from the weekly rewards. They will be able to save some much-needed coins as a result.

One incredibly intriguing 88-rated CM card is available in the Marcel Sabitzer Fantasy FUT SBC. It’s important to keep in mind that this player’s stats and general performance might get better soon. Both Sabitzer and Manchester United will need to perform well.
